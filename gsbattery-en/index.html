01 · Strategic Rationale

World Cup gives GS Battery a clear and relevant reason to enter consumers’ everyday lives.

For a battery brand, the right narrative is not about “talking football.” It is about powering the journeys that surround the football season. This creates a natural connection with car and motorcycle users, as well as decision-makers in mobility and operational needs.

Attention

The tournament creates a major attention stream

Fans continuously follow match schedules, results, expert commentary, behind-the-scenes stories, and post-match highlights.

Relevance

Batteries are connected to every journey

GS Battery can own the state of being “ready”: a reliable start, a safer ride, and uninterrupted moments of enjoyment.

Trust

The approach must be clear and practical

The plan is structured with clear implementation logic, measurable delivery data, transparent reporting, and easy-to-follow information for multiple levels of decision-making.

Core Idea

Power Every Journey. Be Present at Every World Cup Moment.

The core idea helps GS Battery move beyond a technical product role into a more relatable brand role: a trusted source of power that keeps every vehicle ready for the journeys of the season.

Recommended Communication Role

Rather than claiming football emotions directly, GS Battery should own the meaning behind those emotions: preparation, durability, reliable starts, and peace of mind on the move.

02 · Creative Contextual Approach

The right content, the right moment, the right state of attention.

The creative contextual approach does not force GS Battery into football. It places the brand in high-attention contexts where audiences are most receptive to the message of being ready for every journey.

Pre-match Updates

Appear around match schedules, previews, predictions, team news, and trending topics.

Tournament Coverage

Maintain presence on VTV World Cup content and tournament-related coverage during the peak period.

Highlight Viewing

Be present within highlight videos, where post-match emotions are revisited and shared.

Mobility & Preparation

Connect the message with football-viewing journeys, trips with friends and family, and late-night mobility moments.
